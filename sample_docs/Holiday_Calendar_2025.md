## Notes

1. If a holiday falls on a weekend, no compensatory off is provided.
2. Optional holidays (2 per year) can be claimed from the following list:
   - Eid ul-Fitr
   - Eid ul-Adha
   - Guru Nanak Jayanti
   - Mahashivratri
   - Pongal
   - Onam

3. To apply for optional holidays, submit request 7 days in advance through HR portal.
4. Holiday calendar is subject to change based on government notifications.
