IT Support Guide
================

COMPUTER ISSUES
---------------

Slow Computer:
1. Restart your computer first - this fixes most issues.
2. Close unnecessary applications and browser tabs.
3. Check if Windows updates are pending.
4. If still slow, submit a ticket to IT for hardware check.

Forgotten Password:
1. Click "Forgot Password" on the login screen.
2. Enter your company email address.
3. Check your email for the reset link (check spam folder).
4. If no email received within 10 minutes, contact IT helpdesk at ext. 5555.

VPN Connection Issues:
1. Ensure you are connected to the internet.
2. Close and reopen the VPN client.
3. Try connecting to a different VPN server.
4. Check if your VPN credentials have expired (valid for 90 days).
5. Contact IT if issue persists.

EMAIL PROBLEMS
--------------

Cannot Send Emails:
1. Check your internet connection.
2. Verify the recipient email address is correct.
3. Check if attachment size exceeds 25MB limit.
4. Try using webmail at mail.company.com as alternative.

Missing Emails:
1. Check your spam/junk folder.
2. Verify email rules haven't moved the message.
3. Check if storage quota is full (limit: 50GB).
4. Contact IT to check server-side issues.

SOFTWARE REQUESTS
-----------------

To request new software:
1. Submit a request through the IT portal at it.company.com.
2. Include business justification for the software.
3. IT will review within 3 business days.
4. Approved software will be installed within 5 business days.

Note: Only approved software from the company catalog can be installed.
Unauthorized software installation is a policy violation.

HARDWARE SUPPORT
----------------

For hardware issues (keyboard, mouse, monitor):
- Submit a ticket through the IT portal.
- IT will respond within 24 hours.
- Replacement equipment is provided same-day if available.

For laptop repairs:
- Backup your data before submitting for repair.
- Repairs typically take 3-5 business days.
- Loaner laptops are available upon request.
