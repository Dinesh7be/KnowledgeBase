# Company HR Policy Document

## Leave Policy

### Annual Leave
- All full-time employees are entitled to 21 days of annual leave per year.
- Leave must be requested at least 5 working days in advance.
- Unused leave can be carried forward up to a maximum of 5 days to the next year.
- Leave approval is subject to manager discretion based on project requirements.

### Sick Leave
- Employees are entitled to 10 days of paid sick leave per year.
- A medical certificate is required for absences exceeding 2 consecutive days.
- Unused sick leave cannot be carried forward or encashed.

### Maternity/Paternity Leave
- Female employees are entitled to 26 weeks of paid maternity leave.
- Male employees are entitled to 2 weeks of paid paternity leave.
- Leave must be applied at least 1 month before the expected date.

## Working Hours
- Standard working hours are 9:00 AM to 6:00 PM, Monday to Friday.
- Core hours are 10:00 AM to 4:00 PM when all employees must be available.
- Flexible working arrangements can be discussed with your manager.
- Overtime work requires prior approval from the department head.

## Remote Work Policy
- Employees may work remotely up to 2 days per week with manager approval.
- Remote work requests must be submitted through the HR portal.
- Employees must be available during core hours when working remotely.
- A stable internet connection and proper home office setup are required.

## Code of Conduct
- All employees must maintain professional behavior at all times.
- Harassment of any kind will not be tolerated and may result in termination.
- Confidential company information must not be shared externally.
- Personal use of company equipment should be limited and reasonable.
